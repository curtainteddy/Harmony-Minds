Mental health refers to our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act in daily life.

Mental health is important because it affects every aspect of our lives, including our physical health, relationships, and daily functioning.

You can take care of your mental health by practicing self-care, seeking support, engaging in mindfulness, and maintaining a healthy lifestyle.
